About Me

Hi, I’m Matthieu! I’m a passionate problem-solver, tech enthusiast, and aspiring builder. By day, I manage digital projects and help organizations streamline their workflows; by night, you’ll find me gaming with friends, watching shows like The Bear, or cooking meals that fuel my week.

I love creating order out of chaos—whether it's managing accounts and digital platforms or organizing a game of spike ball in the park. With experience ranging from coordinating events to implementing CRMs, I thrive at the intersection of creativity, tech, and people.

When I’m not working, I enjoy exploring wellness practices like yoga and weightlifting, though I’m still figuring out how to stay consistent with them (progress, not perfection!). In the future, I dream of building a home on a piece of land and finding a fulfilling career that aligns with my personal and financial goals.

Above all, I believe that life is about balancing the things that matter—whether it’s spending time with close friends, learning something new, or just enjoying a good cup of peppermint tea. Feel free to reach out—I’d love to connect!
